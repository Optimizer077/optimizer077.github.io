About

Academic grounding, research collaborations, and applied experience.

I am currently pursuing a Ph.D. in Artificial Intelligence at Kyung Hee University Global Campus, where I am part of the Machine Learning and Visual Computing (MLVC) Lab under the supervision of Professor Sung Ho Bae.

Before that, I worked as a Research Assistant at the Center for Intelligent Systems and Security at the Information Technology University, Lahore, where I contributed to automated malaria detection and nanostructure response estimation.

I completed my Bachelor's degree in Electronics Engineering at the University of Engineering and Technology, Taxila, where I first developed my interest in research-led engineering systems.
