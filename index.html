Ali Haider

alihaiderfarooq07 at gmail dot com

I am a Ph.D. researcher in Artificial Intelligence at Kyung Hee University Global Campus, member of the Machine Learning and Visual Computing (MLVC) Lab under Professor Sung Ho Bae.

Previously a Research Assistant at the Center for Intelligent Systems and Security (CISS), ITU Lahore, under Dr. Qasim Mehmood. B.Sc. in Electronics Engineering from UET Taxila.

My research focuses on generative models, implicit neural representations, vision-language models, and image restoration. I have published at AAAI and in Infrared Physics & Technology, with collaborations at Adobe Research and Télécom Paris.

I enjoy 📖 reading, 🌍 traveling, and 🧑‍💻 building things.

Project Involvement

  • Vision-Language Navigation for Autonomous Systems Ongoing

    Developing a vision-language grounded navigation system for autonomous embodied agents, enabling natural language-guided spatial and semantic reasoning with lightweight VLMs.

  • MRI Acceleration and Reconstruction Ongoing

    In collaboration with AiLive and NYU — diffusion models and multi-modal frameworks for faster, accurate clinical MRI diagnostics.

  • Video Quality Assessment for Streaming Media

    With KEI Media — perceptual video quality benchmarking and no-reference quality assessment metrics for streaming platforms.

  • Microscopic Malaria Detection Research

    Low-cost AI system for microscopic malaria detection with Lahore General Hospital (CISS Global). Virtual staining, image enhancement, and low-resource diagnosis.

  • AI-based Sports Card Grading Acquired by Beckett Media

    Algorithm lead at Due Dilly — automated PSA/Beckett-standard grading via large-scale data curation and visual centering networks. Startup acquired by Beckett Media.

  • Real-time Traffic Light Control System

    NIE Islamabad internship — inductive loop sensors with adaptive signal control based on vehicle speed and time-of-day.
