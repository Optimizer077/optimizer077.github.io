Ali Haider

alihaiderfarooq07 at gmail dot com

I am a Ph.D. researcher in Artificial Intelligence at Kyung Hee University Global Campus, member of the Machine Learning and Visual Computing (MLVC) Lab under Professor Sung Ho Bae.

Previously a Research Assistant at the Global Center for Innovative Systems and Solutions (CISS-Global), ITU Lahore, under Dr. Qasim Mehmood. B.Sc. in Electronics Engineering from UET Taxila.

My research focuses on generative models, implicit neural representations, vision-language models, and image restoration. I have published at AAAI and in Infrared Physics & Technology, with collaborations at Adobe Research and Télécom Paris.

I enjoy 📖 reading, 🌍 traveling, and 🧑‍💻 building things.
